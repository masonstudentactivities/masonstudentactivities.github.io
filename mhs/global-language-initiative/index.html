About this club

GLI is all about learning and teaching languages while also sharing aspects of cultures with other students in Mason. This is the perfect club for you if you want to learn a new language, practice one that you know, teach someone else the languages you know, or learn about other countries and cultures. This club is a great way to make friends while learning a brand new skill. The club offers multiple languages ranging from Spanish to Turkish to Mandarin Chinese to Korean to Arabic to Tamil and Hindi. We aim to share the love of language learning with everyone at Mason! Global awareness and compassion starts here!
