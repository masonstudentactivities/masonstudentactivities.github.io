About this club

An honor society dedicated to members passionate about business!

Purpose of NBHS

The purposes of the National Business Honor Society are to:  promote and recognize academic achievement in business education at the secondary level;  foster and recognize leadership skills and character development;  help members grow ethically and socially by promoting and encouraging an interest in business; and  encourage member involvement in service learning initiatives.

Student Membership Qualifications

In addition to the application process, here are some of the requirements: a student must be a high school junior or senior. The student must have completed or be currently enrolled in his/her third business course. The student must have a minimum standard GPA, on a 4.0 scale, as follows: business courses average-3.5; overall average-3.0.
