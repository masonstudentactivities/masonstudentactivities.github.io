About this club

Are you interested in pursuing a career in medicine? Then join the Mason High School Med Club a club dedicated to giving back to the healthcare field and learning more about professions, work style, and college for the medical field! We have 1-2 meetings per month on Tuesdays in A211 from 2:30-3:30 and snacks will be provided. Join us to make a difference in your community through health sciences.
