About this club

Want to help others with their English? Speakeasy is a conversational tutoring club focused on helping others improve their English speaking and listening skills. This club is a great way to help your local community and make an impact; it is also an opportunity to make new friends and learn things about other cultures and countries. This club takes place during connect time on Wednesdays. If you're interested in being a tutor, feel free to join our Schoology page.
