About this club

The National Computer Science Honor Society is a club that encourages enthusiasm for computer science and promotes and recognizes academic excellence and service among high school computer science students.

How to Join

Be a student in the 9th, 10th, 11th, or 12th grade; - Have attended William Mason High School for at least one semester or currently enrolled; - Have completed at least one semester of rigorous computer science coursework at William Mason High School: Computer Programming I or equivalent (see advisor for clarification) - Have earned at least a B in Computer Programming I based on the grading system of the William Mason High School - Demonstrate service and leadership and must have 2 MHS teacher/coach recommendations.
