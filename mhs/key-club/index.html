About this club

Key Club is a service oriented international organization. It's focus centers on character development through to the community at large. It's mission is to help others in need through a variety of service events and projects. As a team, we work together to make a difference both locally and internationally. We value leadership, character building, and inclusiveness in welcoming those from all backgrounds interested in contributing to the community.
