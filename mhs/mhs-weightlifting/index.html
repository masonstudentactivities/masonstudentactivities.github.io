About this club

This club provides its members with a safe and fun space to work out with friends or a partner in the weight room afterschool.

Why to Join

Are you interested in joining a club where you can work out with your friends from school and build a strong community? Look no further! Our student-run high school weightlifting team is the perfect opportunity for you to strengthen your body, reach your goals, and join a supportive, encouraging community. Our club welcomes people of all skill levels and abilities as we partner up when exercising to create friendships and connect with others. This club provides plenty of opportunities to showcase your hard work. Join us to become part of a club that believes in the power of strength and community.
