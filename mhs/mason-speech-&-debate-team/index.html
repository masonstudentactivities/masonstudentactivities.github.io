About this club

Mason Speech & Debate is a competitive academic organization at MHS that competes in various tournaments throughout the state. With 12 different events to choose from, everyone can find their niche within our team and compete in an event that suits their interest. Regardless of the event chosen, students will vastly increase their public speaking capabilities, confidence, and analytical thinking. Weekly tournaments throughout the winter build high team morale and a strong community of like-minded, passionate students.
