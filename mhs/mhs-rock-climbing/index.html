About this club

The Mason Rock Climbing club introduces students to the unique but challenging sport of Rock Climbing. The club will meet twice a month at Mosaic Climbing, where they members explore the various types of climbing: bouldering, which is a powerful and ropeless form of climbing, toprope, which is an endurance/distance based form of climbing, and speed. Climbers will work in group orientated settings where they collaborate together to crack tough climbs and push their limits!
