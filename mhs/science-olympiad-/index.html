About this club

Science Olympiad is a nation-wide competition that features 23 different science-related events. Our coach always compares it to a "track team for SCIENCE." Teams of 15 students compete in groups of 2 or 3 in various STEM events. They can range from building and engineering to research and laboratory, covering topics surrounding physics, chemistry, biology, and technology. Science Olympiad is a program that promotes strong team bonds, leadership, and above all else, fosters a passion for STEM.
