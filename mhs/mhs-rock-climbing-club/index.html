About this club

Rock climbing is a sport that on its own is about individual growth: becoming stronger, learning better technique, and improving mindset. At its heart, there is no competition but one's own previous efforts. But, not many people rock climb alone. It is a very communal sport, and we hope to capture this in MHS Rock Climbing Club. You don't have to be a great climber to have fun, you just need to be willing to try climbing out. So that means beginners are very much welcome! As for what we do, we do all types of climbing: bouldering, top-rope, auto-belay, and lead climbing.

Where?

We climb at rock climbing gym called Mosaic, which is on Union Cemetery Road.

What do you need to climb?

First, wear athletic clothes that you can move around in. And most importantly, you need to fill out your FinalForms AND fill out Mosaic's Waiver. It is on the top right of their homepage: https://www.mosaicclimbing.com/
