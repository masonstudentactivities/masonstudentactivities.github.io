About this club

In the MHS 3D Printing Club, you will be able to learn how to design 3D models, and how to use a 3D printer to turn your designs into functional products or tools, ranging from complex mechanisms to art pieces. You will learn the differences between different types of filament, such as impact strength, or flexibility. From learning what programs to use, to the science of how a 3D printer works, the MHS 3D Printing Club is the place to go for anything 3D-Printer related. Throughout the year, we hope to have various 3D Printing sales, where you will have the opportunity to sell your own 3D objects.
