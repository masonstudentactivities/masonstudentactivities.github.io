About this club

NCHS members actively engage in, explore, and promote Chinese culture in and outside of the school community.

How To Join

You must be in the course Mandarin II or above to join. Native speakers may also join. Applications for new members are available each spring.

Requirements

Members are expected to attend mandatory meetings and will need 8 total volunteer hours per year. At least 2 volunteer hours must come from tutoring Mandarin students (NCHS will host tutoring sessions).
