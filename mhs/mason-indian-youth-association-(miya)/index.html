About this club

MIYA is a club that is centered around the cultural diversity of India. It is a place to celebrate and appreciate everyone within the Indian community, as well as create a safe space to relax and have fun. By leading activities that are inclusive to all parts of India, we are creating this safe space to people of Indian heritage, along with people interested in the Indian culture.
