About this club

Comet Crochet and Knitting provides a place for students to meet others with a shared interest in crochet or knitting. Members can work on individual crochet/knitting projects, or work on club projects. We give back to the Mason community through donations of crocheted or knitted items to local nonprofits, shelters, and charities. Students with no experience knitting or crocheting but are interested in learning are welcome to join!
