About this club

Our mission is to educate the student body, spread awareness, and take direct action on environmental issues/injustices in hopes of becoming a more eco-friendly and environmentally-conscious Mason.We aim to accomplish this through educational campaigns, social media posts and infographics, hosting events/projects, pledges, and running fundraisers.
