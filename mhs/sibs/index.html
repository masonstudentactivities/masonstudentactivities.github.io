SIBS


About this club

SIBS is a group of carefully selected upperclassmen who provide mentoring to the freshmen class, and awareness of social isolation and inclusion. The SIBS meet their assigned freshmen during a Freshmen Orientation Day, and about 4-5 SIBS are assigned to each freshmen homeroom. The SIBS also welcome and tour new students, perform community service, and engage in events that promote inclusivity and combat social isolation.
