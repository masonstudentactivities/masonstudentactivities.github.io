About this club

Improve your critical thinking skills while learning how Constitutional law works. Members form teams to par­tic­i­pate in an orig­i­nal sim­u­lated trial as witnesses or attorneys for plaintiff or defense . The case in presented in real court­rooms (in Warren, Butler, or Clermont counties). The state fi­nals are held in the Ohio State­house and the win­ner ad­vances to the na­tional com­pe­ti­tion.
