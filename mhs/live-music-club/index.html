About this club

The live music club, AKA the AMs, is a band that plays live shows before school in the front lobby once a month or so, as well as other gigs such as the Talent Show. This club meets from 2:45-4:30 PM on Fridays throughout the school year. All instruments are welcome, but all members should be somewhat skilled on their instrument and have a basic knowledge of music theory. Membership is limited for this club, so auditions for new members are held at the beginning of the school year.
