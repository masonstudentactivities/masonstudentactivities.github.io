About this club

MHS science fair is a competitive event, hosted by the district. Students present their science project results in the form of a report, display board, and/or models that they have created to volunteer judges from the community. Science fair allows students to investigate and compete in science, engineering and/or technology topics that they have personal interest in and allows students to enhance their understanding, research, experimentation skills on a personal level with the opportunity to move on to regional, state or international fair competitions.
