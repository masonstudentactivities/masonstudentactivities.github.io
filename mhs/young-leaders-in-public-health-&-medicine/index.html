About this club

MHS Young Leaders in Public Health and Medicine (YLPHM) is a chapter branch of the National YLPHM. It is a youth medical initiative that aspires to improve our world by providing future physicians and public health officials with the experience, opportunities, and leadership skills they need to succeed. MHS YLPHM helps students with common interests in Medicine and Public Health to help our community.
