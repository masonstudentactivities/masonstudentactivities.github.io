About this club

The MHS Economics Club will be an extension of the YEI (Youth Economics Initiative) chapter. Building upon the strong foundation laid by the YEI, the club aims to provide an immersive and enriching experience for students interested in economics and its multifaceted applications. The club will serve as a hub of intellectual exploration and collaborative engagement, where passionate economic enthusiasts can delve deeper into economic concepts, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and actively contribute to enhancing financial literacy within our school community.
