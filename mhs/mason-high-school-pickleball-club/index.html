About this club

This club will allow students to play pickleball once a week at the Pickle Lodge in West Chester throughout the entire school year. We will be playing every Wednesday from 4-5pm. Cost is $10 per player. Sign up is first come first serve basis. Transportation is NOT provided. Final Forms must be completed before participation. Instruction is provided at an extra cost.

How to register/pay

You will be emailed directions on how to register with The Pickle Lodge. Payment will be done through them and not the school.
