About this club

Homework, Examinations, and other assignments are some of common stressors in the average high school student. Humor Club is a place where stress and anxiety no longer exist. It’s a friendly place where members can perform stand up comedy, create memes, and just simply tell jokes to let their stress out. We meet 1-2 times a month on Fridays to relieve stress and come together as MHS. We also have many volunteering outreach events all throughout the year.
