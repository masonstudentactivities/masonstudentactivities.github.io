About this club

At the MHS Baking Club, we discuss fun recipes, exciting baking tricks, and share baking advice in a casual, supportive environment! At every meeting, we share a unique theme, and members will have 4 weeks to bake a delicious item based on that theme. At the following meeting, we will taste what everyone has made.
