About this club

Attention all MMS students! Are you a 7th or 8th grader interested in learning and competing in various fields of science? Science Olympiad is the perfect team for you! Featuring events ranging from Life Sciences, Earth & Space, Physics and Chemistry, to Technology & Engineering, our club has something for everyone. Science Olympiad gives members a chance to discover exciting new fields of science, all while making new friends, and having fun/working together with teammates at meetings! Our team attends high-level competitions (Regionals, States, possibly Nationals), and you’ll be sure to make countless memories you’ll never forget.
